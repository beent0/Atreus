Homeserver Android & Linux Integration

Since this app is served directly from your homeserver, it operates as a full Progressive Web App (PWA) with native-level integration.

Android Native App

  1. Open your Chrome or Firefox browser on Android.
  2. Navigate to your homeserver's address (e.g. http://your-server-ip:8000/).
  3. Tap the three dots menu in Chrome, and select "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App".
  4. The app will install directly in your app drawer, launch in full screen (no browser address bar), and enable push notifications/reminders!

Linux Desktop App

  1. Open Chrome, Edge, or Brave on your Linux machine.
  2. Navigate to the local address (e.g. http://localhost:8000/).
  3. Click the Install Icon in the right-hand corner of the address bar.
  4. This registers a local .desktop entry, adding it to your application menu with full OS taskbar integrations!
